Larry Page's Life

Larry Page was born in 1973 in East Lansing, Michigan. He grew up in a family of computer science professors, which likely influenced his interest in technology from a young age. Page studied computer engineering at Stanford University, where he met Sergey Brin. Lawrence Edward Page is an American businessman, computer engineer and computer scientist best known for co-founding Google with Sergey Brin in 1998.
